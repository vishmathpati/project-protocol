# Brand Foundation

Build brand truth, not visual tokens or marketing pages.

## Read before asking

Read BRIEF, STRUCTURE, existing BRAND, WONT-DO, and relevant TASTE. Reuse project identity, users, problem, business context, stage, parts, and scope. Never ask those universal questions again.

## Ask only brand gaps

Resolve differentiation, credible alternatives, emotional before/after, personality and voice, cultural context and scripts, trust stakes, memorability, distinctive/protected assets, reference tribe, and brand-specific refusals. Use plain language and one focused question at a time.

Do not ask colors, fonts, research depth, pages, sections, layouts, or implementation.

## Diagnose and confirm

Silently derive trust temperature, cultural anchor, category flexibility (`constrained | mixed | open`), primary/secondary archetype, and distinctive-asset status (`established | candidate | unresolved`). Use frequency and density from Foundation/STRUCTURE. Present a concise diagnosis for correction. Category-code ratios are heuristics, never laws. Do not invent real reference websites; UI Research verifies them live.

## Write BRAND only

Preserve researched content. Write product/niche context, audience, problem, differentiation, emotional transformation, personality/voice, cultural context, trust stakes, archetype, reference tribe, distinctive assets/status, memorability, protected elements, refusals, and dated decisions.

BRAND.md follows the structure of the shipped `templates/BRAND.md`.

Do not write DESIGN, marketing, research, chapters, or code. Recommend the appropriate next doorway without auto-chaining.
