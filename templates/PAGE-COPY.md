The copy contract for one page or page family. Lives at `brain/marketing/copy/<page-or-family-id>.md`. For a page family, list every route in backticks in the H1. One section per content job, keyed by the Job ID defined in the page brief.

## <page-id>--job-<slug> — <human-readable name>

| Element | Copy | Tag |
|---|---|---|

Tag vocabulary:

- `(verified)` — reused verbatim from canon or proof.
- `[NEW — client sign-off]` — drafted, needs human approval.
- `[phase-2]` — post-launch swap.

## Rules

- Stage A drafts are organized by content jobs ONLY. Never organize by visual section — hero, band, carousel, and similar layout naming is forbidden before an approved site direction exists.
- Stage B (after the approved site direction) may add presentation notes, but keeps the content-job Job IDs as the section keys.
- Every copy line is an editable proposal. The human may rewrite anything, and human edits win.
