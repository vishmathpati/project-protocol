# Marketing Media

> Format: project-protocol/media.v1

| ID | Page/section job | Type | What it must communicate | Role/spec | Source route | Rights/provenance | Current path | Availability / next action | Stage | Replacement gate |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Source routes: `owned/client-supplied` · `commissioned` · `ai-generated` · `licensed-stock/render` · `neutral-placeholder` · `reference-only` · `not-needed`.

Stages: `required` · `awaiting-source` · `prototype-ready` · `final-ready` · `blocked` · `retired`.

Record aspect/orientation, focal/text-safe area, responsive crop, alt, and treatment in Role/spec. For video also record duration, poster, autoplay/muted/loop, sound, controls, mobile behavior, reduced-motion fallback, and hosting.

Use real client/customer/product assets where available. AI or stand-ins may prove composition, but must not fabricate a real property, product, customer, or proof. Research references are never implementation assets. Never assume redistribution or commercial-use rights.
